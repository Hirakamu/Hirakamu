About

Over the past two years I've quietly become the person who builds and maintains the digital side of my school, SMAN 2 Cikarang Pusat — the website, an LMS frontend, backend services, and a mobile app for the school's digital innovation program. None of it started as a class project; it started because something needed to exist and I was the one willing to build it.

Outside of school work, I run my own Minecraft server node network, keep a growing "script bank" of setup and automation tooling, and build small bots for communities I'm part of. I'm comfortable moving between TypeScript, Python, C++, Dart, and shell — whatever the problem actually needs.
